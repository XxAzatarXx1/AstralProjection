What is Astral Projection?

Astral Projection is an intenyional out-of-body experience where one's consciousness seems to seperate from the physical body, traveling outside to explore other realms or dimensions.

How Does Astral Projection Work?

Astral Projection involves using mental focus and meditation techniques to reach a relaxed state, allowing one to explore consciousness beyond the physical self.

Steps to Achieve Astral Projection

  • Focus on Leaving the Physical Body
  • Visualization and Intent Setting
  • Entering the Astral Plane
